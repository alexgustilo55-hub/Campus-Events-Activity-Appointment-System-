Our Mission


CampusConnect is a campus event and activity appointment system designed to streamline event
discovery, scheduling, and participation enhancing student engagement and campus life.

A system that removes barriers to campus event participation by simplifying scheduling,
registration, and management for students, staff, and organizations.
